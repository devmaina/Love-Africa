Sankofa -- Sankofa is a word in the Akan language of Ghana that translates to "go back and get it" or "reach back and get it."

What is the importance of Sankofa? In Akan language, Sankofa is expressed as “so wo were fi na wosan kofa a yenki”. The translation of this African proverb in English language expresses
“it is not taboo to go back and fetch what you forgot”. Sankofa teaches us the importance of learning from the past to build the future.
